## Create from a template

_<u>Estimated time: 1 minute</u>_

If you are unsure where to start, templates make it easy to get going right away. Just choose a template and customize it to fit your needs.

Follow the steps in this guide to create a delicate PPT of Starbucks:

    Choose **a template**

    ![Create Template](/images/create_template.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Revising with agent" stepNumber={2}>

  </Step>
