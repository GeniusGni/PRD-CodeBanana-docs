Your account settings bring together everything that is personal to you, from your name and profile image to how you send messages in chats. Use this page to keep your details accurate and safe.

## **Accessing your account settings**

You can manage all your personal account options from the profile menu.

**To open your account settings:**

1. Enter the main page of CodeBanana.
2. Click the profile picture in the upper right corner.

### Change profile picture

Click on your profile picture to access the profile picture editing page. Here you can upload an image to change your profile picture.

The profile picture must meet the **following requirements**:

1. Only JPG, PNG, GIF, and WebP formats are supported.
2. Less than 5MB.

### Usage

Click the "Usage" button in your personal center to view your usage details. Overall billing is divided into **two types**:

1. **Package-based requests:**

- Different membership levels—Free, Personal, and Professional—offer 30, 300, and 1000 requests respectively.
- The Personal and Professional packages allow you to choose different virtual machine options based on your service scale; billing will be based on the virtual machine service used.

2. **Pay-as-you-go**

- Once you've used up your package-based requests, you can enable pay-as-you-go billing on the Usage page, setting a limit. This will automatically trigger a Stripe billing process for top-up.

### Contacts

You can view your organization application records, organization members, and external contacts.

The entry to **Contacts** depends on whether **Standard Mode** is enabled.

**Standard Mode On**

When **Standard Mode** is enabled, **Contacts** is available directly from the left navigation bar.

**Standard Mode Off (Classic Layout)**

When **Standard Mode** is disabled, open the profile menu in the upper-right corner, then click **Contacts**.

#### Organization Contacts

Members who have joined the same organization. You can:

- Add them directly to projects without sending email invitations
- Browse and search all organization members in one place
- View each member’s role (Owner / Admin / Member)

#### External Contacts

Collaborators invited by email who are not part of the organization. They become external contacts after accepting an invitation sent either from a project or through **Contacts → External Contacts → Add External Contact**. You can:

- Invite them to projects via email without adding them to the organization
- Find and reuse them when inviting to other projects
- Distinguish them visually from internal members to avoid confusion
- View invitation records and statuses in **History**

|  | **Organization Contacts** | **External Contacts** |
| --- | --- | --- |
| How they join | Join the organization directly | Invited to a project via email |
| Add to project | Can be added directly (no email required) | Requires email invitation |
| Organization member | Yes | No |

- **Admin and owner** can manage the organization structure and members, like inviting members and setting admins.

### **Language Setting**

CodeBanana supports multiple interface languages, allowing you to work in the language you're most comfortable with.

Currently supported languages:

- **English**
- **简体中文 (Chinese)**

To change the interface language:

1. Open the profile menu.
2. Click **Language**.
3. Select your preferred language. The interface updates immediately after the new language is selected.

<Note>
  Your language preference is saved to your account and will be used the next time you sign in.
</Note>

### **Standard Mode**

CodeBanana provides two workspace layouts for different ways of working.

To switch layouts:

1. Open the profile menu.
2. Turn **Standard Mode** on or off.

<Frame>
  ![0715 Switch Standardmode](/images/0715-switch-standardmode.png)
</Frame>

#### **Standard Mode On (Default)**

When **Standard Mode** is enabled, CodeBanana uses the **new workspace layout**. This layout is optimized for **AI-first collaboration**, making conversations with agents more prominent while keeping your files and workspace visible.

It is recommended for users who frequently work with **Team Agent** or **Private Ask**. Features include:

- More space for agent conversations
- Better visibility of agent status and execution progress
- Optimized for AI-assisted coding and collaborative workflows

<Frame>
  ![0715 Standardmodeon](/images/0715-standardmodeon.png)
</Frame>

#### **Standard Mode Off (Classic Layout)**

When **Standard Mode** is disabled, CodeBanana switches to the **classic workspace layout**. The classic layout allocates more space to the editor and file viewer, making it easier to focus on project content. It is recommended for users who primarily read, edit, or review files.

Features include:

- Larger editor and preview area
- More space for project content
- Reduced emphasis on the chat panel for a more document-focused experience

<Frame>
  ![0715 Standardmodeoff](/images/0715--standardmodeoff.png)
</Frame>

<Note>
  You can switch between the two layouts at any time. This setting only changes the workspace layout and does not affect your projects, conversations, or files.
</Note>
